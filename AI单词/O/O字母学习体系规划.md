## 🎯 学习重点分析

### 高频考试词汇
- **核心词汇**：of, on, one, only, open, order, other, out, over, own
- **语法重点**：obey, object, obtain, offer, operate, oppose, organize
- **搭配重点**：of course, on time, one by one, only if, open up, in order, other than, out of, over and over, own up

### 易混淆词汇
- **of/off**：介词vs副词
- **one/once**：数词vs副词
- **on/only**：介词vs副词
- **order/organize**：命令vs组织
- **opposite/oppose**：形容词vs动词
- **owe/own**：欠债vs拥有

### 语法应用重点
- **介词用法**：of, on, off, onto, over, out
- **代词用法**：one, oneself, other, our, ours, ourselves
- **动词用法**：obey, object, obtain, offer, operate, oppose, organize, owe, own

### over系列复合词（单元5-6）
- **位置关系**：over, overall, overhead
- **动作词汇**：overcome, overtake, overthrow, overwhelm, overlook
- **时间空间**：overnight, oversea(s)
